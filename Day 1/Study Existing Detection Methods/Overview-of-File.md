# Objective

## Research existing solutions for **wearable tonic-clonic seizure detection** and understand what previous studies have discovered.

## What I Am Studying :- I am reviewing research papers to learn:

- How tonic-clonic seizures are detected using wearable devices.
- What sensors are commonly used (accelerometer, gyroscope, EMG, etc.).
- What algorithms or methods researchers use to identify seizure movements.
- The strengths and limitations of existing detection systems.
  
## Goal of This Study :- The goal of reviewing these papers is to understand:
- Existing detection approaches
- Sensor technologies used
- Current challenges in seizure detection systems
- Opportunities for improving wearable detection methods
